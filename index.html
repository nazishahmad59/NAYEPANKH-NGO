Welcome to
NayePankh Foundation

UP GOVT. | 80G & 12A Registered

We are one of the Biggest Student led NGO of India with its operations extended in the city of Kanpur, Ghaziabad and various other cities.

"NayePankh Foundation promotes a culture of kindness and strives to instill a sense of giving back to society among today's youth."

"NayePankh Foundation works with a vision to create a society where every child can reach their full potential and enjoy equality in its truest sense."

"NayePankh Foundation has been working since 2021 to support underprivileged and marginalized communities in the areas of hunger relief, sanitation, healthcare, education, awareness, and human rights."
