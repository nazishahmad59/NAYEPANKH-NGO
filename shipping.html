Shipping and Exchange Policy

1. Shipping and Delivery Terms

1.1 Non-Commercial Platform: NayePankh Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. We do not sell goods, products, or commercial items on this website. Therefore, standard commercial shipping and tracking services do not apply to regular website interactions.

1.2 Physical Receipts & 80G Certificates: If a donor explicitly requests a printed or physical copy of their donation receipt or 80G tax exemption certificate, it will be dispatched via standard Indian postal services or trusted domestic couriers.

1.3 Delivery Timelines: Requested physical documents are processed within 7-10 working days from the date of verification and delivered according to local courier timelines. Digital receipts are sent instantly via email.

2. Donation Allocations and Exchanges

2.1 Strict No-Exchange Policy: Donations made to specific campaigns (such as food distribution, education setups, stray animal care, or sanitary napkin distributions) are immediate and final.

2.2 Irreversible Allocations: Funds once transferred cannot be converted, swapped, or "exchanged" for another cause or redirected to a different program at a later stage, as the capital is instantly locked for social procurement.

2.3 Token of Appreciation / NGO Merchandise: In instances where specific donation drives offer a token of appreciation (such as dynamic certificate frames or branded awareness wristbands), these are sent strictly based on availability and cannot be exchanged for cash or alternative items.

3. Lost or Damaged Documentations

3.1 Courier Issues: The Foundation will not be held liable for any document delays or misplacements caused directly by third-party logistics or incorrect addresses provided by the donor.
